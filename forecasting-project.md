# Create a New Forecasting Project

---

## Project Name

University Policy Sentiment Analysis

---

## Simulation Requirement

**Scenario:** If this university strictly enforces the new campus management policy, how will campus public opinion evolve over the next 30 days?

**Key Questions to Simulate:**

1. **Initial Reaction Phase (Days 1–7):**  
   How will students, faculty, and staff react immediately after the strict enforcement announcement? Will there be a spike in negative sentiment on social media and campus forums?

2. **Adaptation Phase (Days 8–21):**  
   Will the community gradually accept the policy, or will organized resistance (petitions, protests) emerge? How do influencer students or faculty opinion leaders shape the narrative?

3. **Stabilization Phase (Days 22–30):**  
   What is the long-term equilibrium of public sentiment? Does the policy become normalized, or does dissent persist and grow?

**Variables to Track:**
- Sentiment polarity (positive / neutral / negative)
- Volume of discussion across platforms (WeChat groups, Weibo, Zhihu, campus BBS)
- Key topics and emotional intensity
- Influence of opinion leaders vs. anonymous voices
